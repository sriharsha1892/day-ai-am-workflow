# /research-account

Build AM-ready account understanding for outreach.

## Required Inputs

- `domain` or Day AI account motion ID.

Optional:

- `account_name`
- `aliases`
- `research_depth`
- `target_personas`

## Reads

- Public account information.
- myRA product context from product.ask-myra.ai positioning.
- Day AI account context, existing notes, actions, pages, and opportunity state.
- Freshsales account/contact/deal/activity/conversation/notes context when available.

## AM Decision Point

Ask the AM to confirm or edit the account-specific use cases and target persona focus before using the research for outreach.

## Day AI Writes

- Account plan/context with:
  - how the organization works,
  - structure and relevant units,
  - myRA-fit use cases,
  - account-specific signals,
  - buyer hypothesis,
  - outreach context,
  - source links/evidence.

## Output

- Concise account brief.
- Signal/use-case table.
- Recommended persona priorities.
- Open questions the AM should answer before outreach.

## Done Criteria

- AM has enough account understanding to map contacts and draft outreach without starting from scratch.
